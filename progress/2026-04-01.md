## Per-Venue Fee Configuration in Brain

### Problem

`ArbDetector` was applying `min-spread-bps` to the raw spread. A cross that clears the raw threshold
but is unprofitable after taker fees would still be emitted as a signal and forwarded to the exec
layer. Brain had no knowledge of venue fees.

### Solution

- `ArbCross` struct gains a `net_spread_bps` field (fee-adjusted spread).
- `ArbDetector` stores a `std::unordered_map<std::string, double> venue_fee_bps_` and a private
  `fee_for_(venue)` helper that returns 0 if a venue is not in the map (backward compatible).
- In `scan()`: `net_spread_bps = spread_bps - sell_fee_bps - buy_fee_bps`. The `min-spread-bps`
  filter is applied to `net_spread_bps`. `max-spread-bps` cap remains on raw spread (data quality
  guard, not profit filter).
- `emit_()` log updated: `spread={}bps net={}bps`.
- JSONL output gains `"net_spread_bps"` field.

### CLI / config

`--venue-fees binance:10,okx:8,bybit:10,kucoin:10,bitget:10`

Parsed by `parse_venue_fees()` in `BrainCmdLine.hpp`. Brain logs all configured fees at startup.
Commented example added to `config/brain.conf`.
